About Dan

Hello, I'm Dan. People around Fernandina, Amelia Island, and Yulee call me the Family Man because family values and reliable craftsmanship sit at the absolute heart of everything I do.

I specialize in premium custom woodworking (built-in bookshelves, decorative drop-and-stops, custom barn doors) and precision installations (structural ceiling beams, fans, mudroom cabinetry). I believe that home improvements should be built to last a lifetime, utilizing tight joint craftsmanship and careful detail.

Unlike large commercial construction crews with massive markups and cluttered scheduling, I deal with you directly, providing honest, customized quotes and clear, step-by-step progress tracking for every build phase.
